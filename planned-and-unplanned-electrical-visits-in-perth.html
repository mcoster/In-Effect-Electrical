What should you do if there’s an electrical fault at home?

Many homes in Perth still rely on outdated switchboards that struggle with today's appliance usage. Old fuse boxes, originally built to handle basic needs like lighting, a refrigerator, and a stove, aren't equipped to support the wide range of devices used in most households now. Today, a typical family might have multiple TVs, phones, computers, air-conditioning, and even EV chargers or pool pumps. This increase in demand can quickly overwhelm an outdated switchboard, potentially leading to issues like blown fuses or even fire risks. In addition, older switchboards often contain asbestos, a hazardous material that should be professionally removed to protect your health.
