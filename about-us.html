About In Effect Electrical

Company Overview
In Effect Electrical, based in Malaga, is a trusted provider of electrical and data cabling services across Greater Perth. Specializing in residential, commercial, and industrial sectors, the company brings a commitment to quality and expertise in all things electrical. Known for its innovative approach to modern lighting solutions and smart technologies, In Effect Electrical has built a reputation on customer satisfaction and technical precision.

Specialty in Lighting Solutions
Lighting is one of In Effect Electrical’s key focus areas. They not only provide standard lighting installations but also offer customized lighting designs that enhance aesthetics, improve security, and add convenience for residential and commercial clients alike. With advancements in smart home technology, In Effect Electrical enables clients to control their lighting remotely through smartphones, making lighting management convenient and efficient. Their team of electricians brings expertise in these advanced installations, offering clients state-of-the-art lighting solutions that are both functional and stylish.

For customers interested in learning more about remote lighting options, In Effect Electrical offers a detailed “remote lighting” page that highlights various modern solutions. By choosing In Effect Electrical for lighting projects, customers benefit from the team’s experience, attention to detail, and dedication to achieving the perfect lighting ambiance.

Residential, Commercial, and Industrial Services
In Effect Electrical covers a wide array of services for all types of properties:

  • Domestic Services: From small repairs to major installations, the team is equipped to handle residential projects, ensuring quality work and a tidy workspace.
  • Commercial Services: For businesses, In Effect Electrical provides expert electrical setups, including office lighting, data cabling, and other essential infrastructure. They work efficiently to minimize disruption and maintain a clean workspace.
  • Industrial Services: Large-scale facilities rely on In Effect Electrical for comprehensive electrical solutions that prioritize safety, durability, and efficiency. Their services include LED high bay lighting installations, emergency machine repairs, and more.

Licenses, Certifications, and Insurance
